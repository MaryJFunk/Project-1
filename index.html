EVENING WINE

A Glass of Wine

We drink wine after a long day. When we eat with dinner. Wine relaxes our mind and makes us happy. Drinking wine moderately can be good for your mental happiness and physical health. Many new studies are backing up the heath benefits of drinking wine moderately every day.

From attacking cancer cells to decreasing the mortality rate a little wine in the evening can go a long way to benefiting our health. Moderate drinking means a 5-ounce glass of wine. For women, it is one glass per day and for men it is two glasses per day. Go ahead and drink a glass of wine and look at the ten different ways wine benefits your health.

Did you know the ten benefits?

  • Contains antioxidants
  • Boosts the immune system
  • Increases bone density
  • Reduces the risk of stroke
  • Reduces the risk of heart disease
  • Can Lower cholesterol
  • Reduces the risk of type 2 diabetes
  • Reduces the risk of cancer
  • Improves cognitive function
  • Promotes longevity
